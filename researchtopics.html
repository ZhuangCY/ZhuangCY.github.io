          I am currently conduting two research projects: (1) Sightseeing Research Project to meet the growing diverse demands for tours; and (2) Intelligent City to make a city have intelligence. The essentials of these two projects are to analyze the Human-Location-Interaction (HLI).


1. Sightseeing Research Project

By dividing sightseeing locations into four quadrants on the basis of their “popularity” and “sightseeing quality”, this research mainly aims to discover obscure POIs that are located in the quadrant with high sightseeing quality and low popularity.

2. Intelligent City

In this project, I am aiming to construct an intelligent ecosystem for solving different problems encountered in a city. Under the current framework, it consists of 3 main steps:

(1) Knowledge Leveraging. This subtopic aims to leverage the distributed heterogeneous raw data collected from the real world into knowledge (e.g., knowledge bases);

(2) Knowledge Embedding. Given knowledge bases(graphs), this subtopic aims to devise convolutional methods to make computers better understand the knowledge leveraged from raw data;

(3) Applications. This subtopic aims to devise different machine learning models as a cloud to solve a particular social problem.
